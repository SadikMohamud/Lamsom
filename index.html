About the Lambeth Somali Community Association

The Lambeth Somali Community Association (Lamsom, for short) supports the Somali community in Lambeth by offering bilingual advice and advocacy, primarily for families, women, and young people. Key initiatives include a supplementary school providing academic support in core subjects like math, English, and science. While most beneficiaries are women and youth, the charity increasingly assists older individuals and single parents, addressing their unique needs and promoting their well-being.

Stockwell F.C

The Lambeth Somali Community Association (LamSom) is a community-centered organization based in Stockwell, Southwest London, dedicated to uplifting and empowering the local Somali community. Among its key initiatives is Stockwell F.C., a football team that provides a platform for youth and adults to connect through sport, fostering teamwork, discipline, and personal growth. LamSom serves as a vital hub for cultural enrichment and social engagement, championing social cohesion, celebrating Somali heritage, and promoting inclusivity. Together with Stockwell F.C., the association strengthens community bonds and inspires a shared vision for a brighter, united future.

LamSom Community Event Days Celebrating Unity and Heritage

Community Event Days hosted by the Lambeth Somali Community Association are vibrant celebrations that bring together individuals of all ages. These events feature engaging cultural showcases, immersive workshops, spirited storytelling sessions, and activities that honor Somali heritage. Attendees can enjoy traditional cuisine, and recreational activities, fostering a sense of unity and strengthening community bonds.

LamSom Supplementary School (Record Of Achievement Ceremony)

The Lambeth Somali Community Association (LamSom) supplementary school provides academic support in core subjects such as mathematics, English, and science, tailored to meet the needs of young learners. Recently, the school celebrated a milestone with its Record of Achievement Ceremony, honoring the hard work and dedication of its students. The event showcased their remarkable progress and commitment, bringing together families and the community to celebrate academic excellence and foster a culture of learning. LamSom remains dedicated to empowering the next generation with education as a cornerstone for success.
