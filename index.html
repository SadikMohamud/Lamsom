About the Lambeth Somali Community Association

The Lambeth Somali Community Association (LAMSOM, for short) supports the Somali community in Lambeth by offering bilingual advice and advocacy, primarily for families, women, and young people. Key initiatives include a supplementary school providing academic support in core subjects like math, English, and science. While most beneficiaries are women and youth, the charity increasingly assists older individuals and single parents, addressing their unique needs and promoting their well-being.

Projects and Services Available at LAMSOM

Advice & Employment Support

LAMSOM offers tailored advice and employment assistance, helping individuals with job applications, CV writing, and career planning to achieve economic stability and personal growth.

Cancer Screening & Early Detection Awareness Project

Educating the community about cancer prevention, screening, and early detection to improve health outcomes and promote timely medical intervention.

Stockwell Football Club

A platform for youth and adults to foster teamwork, discipline, and community spirit through sports, inspiring healthier lifestyles and stronger social bonds.

Child & Mother Immunisation Awareness Project

Raising awareness about the importance of immunizations, this project focuses on promoting health education and ensuring mothers and children access necessary vaccines.

LAMSOM Supplementary School

Providing academic support in core subjects like English, mathematics, and science, this program helps students achieve their educational goals and reach their full potential.

Women's Group Project

A dedicated space for women to connect, share experiences, and engage in activities promoting empowerment, skill-building, and community support.

Stockwell F.C

The Lambeth Somali Community Association (LAMSOM) in Stockwell, Southwest London, empowers the local Somali community through initiatives like Stockwell F.C., fostering teamwork, discipline, and growth. LAMSOM serves as a hub for cultural enrichment, social cohesion, and inclusivity, strengthening bonds and celebrating Somali heritage.

LAMSOM Community Event Days Celebrating Unity and Heritage

Community Event Days by the Lambeth Somali Community Association are lively gatherings for all ages. They feature cultural showcases, workshops, storytelling, and activities celebrating Somali heritage. Attendees enjoy traditional cuisine and recreational activities, fostering unity and strengthening community bonds.
